BACHELORS IN MASS MEDIA

ELIGIBILITY
A candidate for being eligible for admission to the three year degree course in Bachelor of Mass Media shall have passed the H.S.C. (Std. XII) examination of Maharashtra State Board of Higher Secondary Education or its equivalent from Science, Arts or Commerce Stream.
