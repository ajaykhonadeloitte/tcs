BACHELORS IN MASS MEDIA

ELIGIBILITY
A candidate for being eligible for admission to the three year degree course in Bachelor of Mass Media shall have passed the H.S.C. (Std. XII) examination of Maharashtra State Board of Higher Secondary Education or its equivalent from Science, Arts or Commerce Stream.
PROGRAM STRUCTURE
FY BMM PROGRAM

SEMESTER I

  • Effective Communication Skills–I
  • Fundamentals Of Mass Communication
  • Introduction To Computers
  • Landmark Events In 20th Century History
  • Introduction To Sociology
  • Introduction To Economics

SEMESTER II

  • Effective Communication Skills–I
  • Political Concepts And The Indian Political System
  • Principles Of Management And Marketing
  • Introduction To Psychology
  • Translation Skills
  • An Introduction To Litterateurs
